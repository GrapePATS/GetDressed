How to dress for

Hourglass

Tips
  • Define the waist

    • -Wear darker colours around your waist area - such as a dark belt

    -Accentuate the waist with belts or nipped garments

  • Follow the natural silhouette

    • - Choose clothing that shows off your curvy silhouette

    - Invest in a good bra

    - Avoid shapeless and loose clothing as well as eye-catching embellishments

    Tops

    The best type of sleeve is a fitted one.Tops must not unbalance the natural silhouette of the hourglass body Avoid big tops that would add a lot of volume to your upper body.

    belted tops
    peplum tops
    wrap tops
    sleeveless tops
    Skirts

    Make sure your skirt is high-waisted to highlight the small waist.The best hemline is knee-length or longer. These skirts will hug the hips and hang off them naturally.

    full skirts
    A-line skirts
    gored skirts
    pencil skirts
    Trousers

    Wide-legged and loose-fitting trousers that go straight down to the floor are best.Trousers should be mid-waisted or3 low-waisted styles would widen the hips.

    bootcut trousers
    wide trousers
    slim jeans
    straight trousers
    Dress

    Dresses should draw the focus to the waist without adding extra volume to the bust.Nipped and belted dresses are excellent. Wrap dresses the best!
