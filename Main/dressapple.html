How to dress for

Apple

Tips
  • Define the waist

    • - Wear darker colours around your waist area such as a dark belt.

    - Accentuate the waist with belts or nipped garments.

  • Follow the natural silhouette

    • - Invest in a good bra

    - Choose clothing that shows off your curvy silhouette

    - Avoid shapeless and loose clothing as well as eye-catching embellishments

    Tops

    Draw the eye away from the midsection and towards the bust and shoulders,make sure tops is long enough to cover the tummy.

    Skirts

    Skirts for the apple shape body should disguise the wider mid-section.Straight skirts with a flirty flared bottom look good.Avoid ruffled skirt styles that would bulk the tummy area.

    asymmetric skirts
    trumpet skirts
    bias skirts
    bubble skirts
    Trousers

    Trousers for the apple-shaped body should aim to minimise the lower body.Trousers should be flat-fronted and should not cling to the body.

    boyfriend jeans
    cargo trousers
    fllowy trousers
    flare trousers
    Dress

    Dress for the apple body shape skims over the midsection without clinging to the body. A-line dresses, empire-line and bias-cut styles are good for you.
